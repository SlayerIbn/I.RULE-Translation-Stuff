Cafeteria-Black is used for waves messages, Malgun Gothic is the main font.

To create your own font for I.RULE you will need GameMaker Studio (I'm using GameMaker Studio 1.4). When you are ready to create it, use the settings that are in "Settings.jpg".

Also you will need Russian letters: "йцукенгшщзхфывапролджэячсмитьбюъёЙЦУКЕНГШЩЗХФЫВАПРОЛДЖЭЯЧСМИТЬБЮЪЁ"
